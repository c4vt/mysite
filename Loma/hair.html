Tips for hair health

By Cavit - August 30th

This section is focused on people who want to improve hair health.


    Tips:

  • - Limit shampooing. Especially wavy/curly hair, as they require more moisture.

  • - Avoid washing hair in hot water, use lukewarm or semi cold water.

  • - Limit hair products, stick to one Quality Shampoo/Conditioner and optionally use a Hair Oil.

  • - Prevent breakage by minimizing touching hair, and be gentle while drying wet hair. Consider using a Microfiber Towel.

  • - Use Satin Pillowcases, prevent sleeping with damp hair, and consider nightly scalp massages to for hair growth.

  • - Limit sun exposure to your head. Aim for around 15 minutes a day and wear a hat for extended periods outdoors.

  • - Optionally, take biotin daily, biotin helps with hair health, and as always, keep a healthy lifestyle, get good sleep and keep a clean diet.
